Discover SeaGuide

SeaGuide provides valuable tools and resources to help you make sustainable seafood choices while

Our Mission

To create a sustainable future for oceans by promoting responsible seafood consumption, preventing
overfishing, and supporting marine biodiversity through community engagement and education.

  • Promote sustainable seafood choices.

  • Raise awareness about the impact of
    overfishing.

  • Provide a reliable reporting system
    for unethical fishing practices.
