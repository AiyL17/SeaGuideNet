Your Questions Answered

Sustainable SeaFood FAQs

Find answers to common questions about sustainable seafood practices and how you can contribute.

What is Sustainable Seafood?

Sustainable seafood is sourced in ways that consider the long-term vitality of harvested species and the well-being of the oceans.

How can I identify sustainable seafood?

Look for certifications and labels such as MSC or ASC, and consult resources like SeaGuide for regional guidelines.

Why is sustainability important in seafood?

Sustainability ensures the future availability of seafood and the health of marine ecosystems.
